Madeleine K Wyburd

I am a DPhil student in the recently established Oxford Machine Learning NeuroImaging Lab (OMNI) at the University of Oxford, where I am supervised by Dr Ana Namburete and Prof. Mark Jenkinson. I am funded by the UKRI EPRSC/MRC as part of the ONBI DTC.

My research uses artificial intelligence to study fetal brain folding from routine ultrasound scans, with the aim of identifying early biomarkers in diseased groups.
